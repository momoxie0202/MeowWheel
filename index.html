歡迎使用「喵轉盤」（Meow Wheel）。本應用程式由個人開發者 Momoxie 開發。 為了讓您安心使用本 App，本政策將清楚說明我們收集、使用及保護您的資訊方式。

Welcome to “Meow Wheel.” This app is developed by the individual developer Momoxie. To help you use this app with confidence, this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. 我們蒐集哪些資訊？

1. What Information Do We Collect?

喵轉盤不會蒐集任何可識別個人身分的資訊（如姓名、Email、電話、精確位置、照片等）。

Meow Wheel does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email, phone number, precise location, or photos.

為提供正常功能，本 App 僅使用以下資料：

To provide core features, the app only uses the following data:

（1）本地儲存（SharedPreferences）

(1) Local Storage (SharedPreferences)

用途如下：

Used for:

  • 儲存轉盤選項
  • Save wheel options
  • 儲存轉動秒數
  • Save spin duration
  • 儲存轉盤名稱、輪盤列表
  • Save wheel names and wheel lists

以上資料全部僅儲存在您的裝置上，不會上傳到伺服器。

All of this data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

（2）Google AdMob（第三方廣告服務與 SDK）

(2) Google AdMob (Third-Party Ads Service & SDK)

本 App 有安裝並使用第三方廣告 SDK，包含 Google 提供的 Google Mobile Ads SDK（AdMob），用於顯示橫幅或插頁廣告， 以及評估廣告成效與防止無效流量。

This app includes and uses third-party advertising SDKs, including the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob), to display banner or interstitial ads and to measure ad performance and prevent invalid traffic.

AdMob 及其廣告合作夥伴可能依其隱私權政策，自動收集以下類型的資料：

AdMob and its advertising partners may automatically collect the following types of data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • 裝置資訊（例如裝置型號、作業系統版本、廣告 ID 或其他裝置識別碼）
  • Device information (such as device model, OS version, advertising ID, or other device identifiers)
  • 應用程式使用情況與廣告互動資料（如廣告展示次數、點擊情形）
  • App usage and ad interaction data (such as ad impressions and clicks)
  • 依 Google 定義的粗略位置等非精確資訊
  • Approximate location or similar non-precise information as defined by Google

這些資料由 Google 及其合作夥伴依其政策處理與保存，本 App 不會看見或識別個別使用者身分。 詳細內容請參考 Google 隱私權政策：
https://policies.google.com/privacy

This data is processed and stored by Google and its partners according to their policies. The app does not access or identify individual users based on this information. For more details, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

若您不希望接收個人化廣告，您可以在裝置的系統設定中重置或限制廣告 ID， 或依照 Google 提供的說明調整廣告偏好設定。

If you prefer not to receive personalized ads, you may reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings or adjust ad preferences following Google’s instructions.

2. 我們不會做的事

2. What We Do Not Do

  • 不會自行建立後端伺服器來收集或儲存您的個人資料
  • We do not create our own backend server to collect or store your personal data.
  • 不會主動要求您提供姓名、Email、電話或其他可識別身分的資訊
  • We do not actively ask you to provide your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information.
  • 不會將您的資料出售給任何第三方
  • We do not sell your data to any third parties.
  • 不會在本地儲存中寫入與您真實身分直接關聯的資訊
  • We do not store any data in local storage that directly links to your real-world identity.

3. 資料安全

3. Data Security

本 App 本地儲存的資料全部保存在您的裝置內，不會透過網路傳輸到自建伺服器。 但請注意，第三方廣告服務（如 Google AdMob）會依其政策在其伺服器上處理資料。

All locally stored data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our own servers. However, third-party ad services (such as Google AdMob) may process data on their servers in accordance with their own policies.

4. 兒童隱私

4. Children’s Privacy

本 App 並非特別設計給 13 歲以下兒童使用，也不會刻意收集兒童的個人資料。 如您是家長或監護人並認為兒童在未經您同意的情況下提供了資料，請與我們聯繫。

This app is not specifically designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided data without your consent, please contact us.
